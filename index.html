Crystal Collector!

How to Play:

  • You will be given a random number everytime you click 'Start Game'
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number generated at the beginning of the game. You lose the game if your total points exceed that number
  • The value of each crystal is unknown until you click on it. When you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change. You're going to have to do the math!
